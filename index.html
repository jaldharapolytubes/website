About Us

Incepted in the year 2017, Jaldhara Polytubes Pvt. Ltd. is counted for offering top class quality Industrial Pipes, Industrial Pipe Fittings, water storage tank, Irrigation Laterals pipe and Agriculture Equipment Sprinklers pipe. We manufacture Pipes from 20mm dia to 450mm dia of all the grades and pressure. The company has attained the heights of success under the proficient headship of the CEO Mr. Abhay Jha, who has 25 years of voluminous experience behind him in this domain. We have been working with Bihar Government since last 3 years and working with Drinking & Water Sanitation Department, Jharkhand since 2 years and a Registered Vendor in PHED, West Bengal.

Infrastructure

Our state – of – the - art infrastructure is spread over more than 73 thousand Sq. We have in-house R&D facility to develop innovative products and Laboratory in order to provide products as per ISI standards. Quality

We give prime importance to the quality of products manufactured by us, as quality is the basic criterion that manages to give us an edge in the market.
